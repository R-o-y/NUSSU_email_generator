NUS Students' Sports Club3

Hey Everyone!


IFG Closing Ceremony will be held on 22 Sept 2017 at NUS newly refurbished University Sports Centre. There will be exciting food booths with LlaoLlao, Maki-San, Churros, Mango Sticky Rice, Thai Milk Tea and many more! Not to forget our lucky draw where lucky winners will be hand-picked during the carnival to win our fabulous prizes! Don't say we didn't mention there is a Staycation and Go-Pro Hero camera up for grabs!!

So what are you waiting for? Gather your friends and purchase our tickets to our carnival! https://orgsync.com/140428/events/2020748/occurrences/4806431

Tickets will be sold at $6 each, if more than 4 tickets are bought, it will be priced at $5.50. Parts of the proceed from the sale of these tickets will be given to NUS Giving.

#IFG2017 #OurTeamOurDreams
